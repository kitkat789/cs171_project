Rebalancing San Francisco’s resources

Data-driven steps can close the opportunity gap. Each recommendation below stems from the patterns exposed in the dashboard.

1 · Target facility investments

Direct new libraries, community centers, and recreation hubs to Districts 1, 4, and 7, where facility counts trail downtown districts by a factor of three.

2 · Require green space in business cores

Tie development approvals in SOMA and the Financial District to the creation of pocket parks or rooftop public plazas to offset the lack of large parks.

3 · Bridge jobs and parks through transit

Expand rapid transit links between park-rich western neighborhoods and central job hubs, cutting travel time for residents who currently commute across the city for work.
